Garbage Grinder

A garbage grinder, with grinding chamber, and rotating blades. is made to solve the problem of not knowing what to do with disposed waste. When food waste is placed inside and the unit is switched on, the motor spins the blades at high speed, breaking the waste into tiny particles. Water flushes the ground waste safely through the plumbing system. This helps reduce trash and the grinded food waste can be cultivated as fertilizer.

  • Hardware
  • Equipments: Battery, CD, LED, Jumper wires
  • Arduino Sketch, Python
